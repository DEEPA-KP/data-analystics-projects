# 🍔 FoodHub Demand & Customer Analytics

## 📊 Business Problem
FoodHub, an online food aggregator, wants to understand customer behavior, order patterns, and factors impacting demand to improve business performance and customer experience.

---

## 🎯 Objective
Analyze customer and order data to:
- Identify key demand patterns  
- Understand customer behavior  
- Evaluate factors affecting orders  
- Provide actionable business insights  

---

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Python (Pandas, NumPy)
- SQL
- Data Analysis & Visualization

---

## 📁 Dataset Overview
The dataset includes:
- Customer orders  
- Restaurant details  
- Order ratings and delivery times  

---

## 🔍 Key Analysis

- Order trends by day and time  
- Popular cuisines and restaurants  
- Customer ratings and satisfaction  
- Delivery time impact on ratings  
- Cost vs order behavior  

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Peak orders occur during specific time windows  
- Certain cuisines dominate demand  
- Delivery time impacts customer ratings  
- High-rated restaurants attract repeat customers  

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Optimize delivery during peak hours  
- Promote high-performing restaurants  
- Improve delivery efficiency  
- Focus on customer satisfaction metrics
