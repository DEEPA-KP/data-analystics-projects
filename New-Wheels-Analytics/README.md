
# 🚗 New Wheels Sales & Customer Analytics

## 📊 Business Problem
New-Wheels, a vehicle resale company, is experiencing a steady decline in sales due to poor customer feedback and reduced new customer acquisition. Leadership requires a quarterly performance report to assess business health and take corrective actions.

---

## 🎯 Objective
Analyze customer, sales, and feedback data using SQL to:
- Understand customer distribution and preferences  
- Evaluate customer satisfaction trends  
- Track revenue and order performance  
- Generate insights for business decisions  

---

## 🛠 Tools Used
- SQL  
- Excel / SQL Client  

---

## 📁 Dataset Overview
The dataset includes:
- Customer information  
- Orders and transactions  
- Vehicle details  
- Customer feedback  

---

## 🔍 Key Analysis

- Customer distribution across states  
- Top vehicle makers preferred by customers  
- State-wise vehicle preferences  
- Customer feedback and rating trends  
- Order trends by quarter  
- Revenue analysis and growth trends  
- Discount analysis  
- Shipping time analysis  

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Sales and revenue show a declining trend  
- Customer dissatisfaction is increasing over time  
- Certain regions contribute more to business performance  
- Popular vehicle makers drive higher engagement  

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Improve after-sales service quality  
- Focus on high-performing regions  
- Optimize pricing and discount strategies  
- Reduce shipping delays  
- Monitor customer feedback regularly
